How to Install on iOS

Install worldwide using Sideloadly or AltStore Classic. Free, simple setup.

Step 1

Setup Tool

On your Windows/Mac computer, download Sideloadly (easiest) or AltStore Classic.

Step 2

Sideload App

Connect your iPhone via USB, drag the .ipa file into the tool, and enter your Apple ID to install. Done!
