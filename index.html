iOS Installation Help

Tap "Install for iOS" on your iPhone and it should install automatically. If it doesn't work, try these:

1

Open in Safari

The install link only works in Safari, not Chrome or other browsers.

2

Trust Certificate

Go to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management and trust the developer.

3

Beta Tester?

This is a beta build. Ask the developer to register your device first.
