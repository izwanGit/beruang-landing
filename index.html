How to Install on iOS

Follow these 3 simple steps to install the app on your iPhone.

Step 1

Get the App

First, download the .ipa file from the main download section on this site.

Step 2

Setup Tool

On your Windows or Mac, download and install Sideloadly.

Step 3

Sideload

Connect your iPhone via USB, drag the .ipa into Sideloadly, enter your Apple ID, and click Start.
