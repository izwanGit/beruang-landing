How to Install on iOS

Since this app is not on the App Store yet, you need to "side-load" the .ipa file.

1

Download .IPA

Download the beruang.ipa file to your computer.

2

Sidebar Tool

Download AltStore or Sideloadly on your PC/Mac.

3

Install

Connect your iPhone, open the tool, and drag the .ipa file into it to install.

* You may need to "Trust" your own developer certificate in Settings > General > VPN & Device Management.
