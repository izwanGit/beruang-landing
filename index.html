How to Install on iOS

Use AltStore to install. Free, 5 min setup, works on any iPhone.

1

Get AltStore

Go to altstore.io on your computer and download it.

2

Setup

Connect your iPhone via cable, follow the guide to install AltStore on your phone.

3

Install App

Download the .ipa file, then open it with AltStore. Done!

* Refresh the app weekly via AltStore to keep it working.
